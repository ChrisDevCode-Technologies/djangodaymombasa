Join the first Django Mombasa community meetup. We will outline our goals for
the year, share what we are building, and open the floor for project pitches.

## Agenda

- Welcome and community overview
- Lightning talks: local Django and Python projects
- Roadmap: workshops, mentorship, and open source
- Networking and project matchmaking

## Who should attend

Developers, designers, and product builders working on Python, Django, and
backend systems. Beginners are welcome.
